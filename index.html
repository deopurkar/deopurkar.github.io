Research

My research area is algebraic geometry. I study moduli spaces: the moduli space of algebraic curves, the Hurwitz space of branched covers, the Severi variety of curves on surfaces, the Kontsevich space of maps and so on. I am also interested in representation theory, number theory, and arithmetic geometry.

Some time ago, I wrote a rough non-technical explanation of my interests.

You can find my papers and preprints on my research page.
